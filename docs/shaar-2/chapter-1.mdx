***Shaar* 2 Chapter 1**

**From Higher World to Lower World**

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:1"
  hebrew="וְהִנֵּה אַחַר שֶׁדִּבַּרְנוּ בְּשַׁעַר א׳ מֵעִנְיַן מְצִיאוּת גִּלּוּיָיו ית״ש אֲשֶׁר נִתְפַּשֵּׁט מֵאֲמִתָּתוֹ הַנֶּעֱלָמָה וְנִתְגַּלָּה בַּאֲצִילוּת וְעַל יְדֵי זֶה הִמְצִיא אֶת הַמְּצִיאוּת דְּעוֹלָמוֹת בי״ע"
  translation="We spoke in the first *shaar* of this work about His revelations[^1] — how they spread forth from His hidden true being and are revealed in Atzilut, and in this way the existence of the worlds of *BiYA* is produced."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:2"
  hebrew="וְהִנֵּה הָיָה סֵדֶר הִתְפַּשְּׁטוּת גִּלּוּיָיו שֶׁבַּאֲצִילוּת בְּדֶרֶךְ הִשְׁתַּלְשְׁלוּת הַמַּדְרֵגוֹת וְיָרַד מִמַּדְרֵגָה לְמַדְרֵגָה עַד שֶׁיָּרַד לְהִתְגַּלוֹת בִּפְעֻלַּת מְצִיאוּת הָעוֹלָמוֹת בי״ע וְהַנְהָגָתָם"
  translation="Now the orderly manner in which this spreading forth of His revelations in Atzilut proceeds is by way of *hishtalshelut hamadreigot* — a chain of cause and effect, descending from level to level, until it descended to where these Divine revelations became revealed in producing the existence of the worlds of *BiYA* and in the manner in which they are conducted."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:3"
  hebrew="וּכְדֵי לְהָבִין קְצָת סֵדֶר הִתְפַּשְּׁטוּת הַמַּדְרֵגוֹת שֶׁבַּאֲצִילוּת סִדְרָם וְקִשּׁוּרָם זֶה בָּזֶה נִלְמַד מֵהַמְּאֻחָר אֶל הַקּוֹדֵם הַיְינוּ מִסֵּדֶר פְּעֻלָּתוֹ ית״ש אֲשֶׁר אָנוּ רוֹאִין בָּעוֹלָם הַזֶּה"
  translation="In order to understand in some small measure the order of this spreading forth of the levels in Atzilut, their sequence, and how they are connected to one another — let us learn in reverse, from later to earlier, that is, from the order of His actions we see in this world and upward."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:4"
  hebrew="וּמִזֶּה נוּכַל לְהָבִין קְצָת אֵיךְ שֶׁהוּא הַסֵּדֶר בְּשָׁרְשָׁם בְּקֹדֶשׁ וּבְקֹדֶשׁ הַקֳּדָשִׁים אֲשֶׁר לְמַעְלָה לְמַעְלָה"
  translation="And from this we will be able to understand a little about the order of things in their root in the holy realms and the holy of holy realms that are above and beyond."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:5"
  hebrew="וְנַתְחִיל וְנֹאמַר כִּי הִנֵּה אָנוּ רוֹאִין בְּכָל הַנִּמְצָאִים כֻּלָּם שֶׁבָּעוֹלָם הַזֶּה הֵן בִּכְלָל וְהֵן בִּפְרָט הִנֵּה מְיֻסָּד כָּל אֶחָד עַל שְׁנֵי בְּחִינוֹת שֶׁהֵם חֹמֶר וְצוּרָה אוֹ גּוּף וְנֶפֶשׁ"
  translation="Let us begin by stating that all the entities we see in this world — in general and in particular, i.e. general categories and individual entities — are all based on two aspects: matter and *tzurah* (form)[^2] — or body and soul."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:6"
  hebrew="וּכְמוֹ שֶׁמְּבֹאָר כָּל זֶה בַּדְּרוּשִׁים הָאַחֲרוֹנִים שֶׁבְּסֵפֶר עֵץ חַיִּים וַעַיֵּן בְּסֵפֶר הַבְּרִית בִּתְחִלָּתוֹ"
  translation="as explained in the last teachings in *Etz Chaim*[^3] — see also *Sefer HaBrit* in the beginning."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:7"
  hebrew="וְהִנֵּה אֵין חֹמֶר בְּלִי צוּרָה וְלֹא צוּרָה בְּלִי חֹמֶר כַּנּוֹדָע"
  translation="Now in our world there is no matter without *tzurah*, and no *tzurah* without matter, as is known."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:8"
  hebrew="וְהַגַּם שֶׁהֵם שְׁנֵי מְצִיאוּיוֹת אֲשֶׁר הֵם רְחוֹקִים בְּמַדְרֵגָתָם זֶה מִזֶּה הַרְבֵּה מְאֹד עִם כָּל זֶה הִנֵּה הֵם מִתְיַחֲדִים זֶה בָּזֶה בְּיִחוּד עָצוּם עַד שֶׁאִי אֶפְשָׁר לְהִמָּצֵא זֶה בְּלֹא זֶה"
  translation="And even though these are two distinct entities, very distant from one another in terms of their levels, they are united with one another in such a powerful unity that it is impossible to find one without the other."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:9"
  hebrew="וְהָעִנְיָן בְּזֶה הוּא כִּי כָּל מָה שֶׁאָנוּ רוֹאִין שֶׁהֵם שְׁתֵּי מַדְרֵגוֹת וְגָבוֹהַּ מַעֲלַת הַצּוּרָה עַל הַחֹמֶר בְּאֵין עֶרֶךְ הִנֵּה כָּל זֶה הוּא רַק בְּהַחֹמֶר וְצוּרָה שֶׁבְּכָל הַנִּמְצָאִים שֶׁבָּעוֹלָם הַזֶּה גּוּפָהּ"
  translation="The concept is this: everything we see as two different levels, where *tzurah* is immeasurably superior to matter, is all exclusively as regards matter and *tzurah* as found in this world itself."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:10"
  hebrew="וְכֵן בְּכָל הַנִּמְצָאִים שֶׁבְּכָל עוֹלָם וְעוֹלָם עַצְמוֹ"
  translation="Similarly with all the entities in each of the worlds themselves — *tzurah* and matter are inseparably united."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:11"
  hebrew="אֲבָל מֵעוֹלָם לְעוֹלָם הִנֵּה נַעֲשָׂה מֵהַחֹמֶר שֶׁל עוֹלָם הָעֶלְיוֹן הַצּוּרָה שֶׁל עוֹלָם הַתַּחְתּוֹן"
  translation="But from world to world — from a higher plane of existence to the one below it — the matter of the higher world becomes the *tzurah* of the lower world."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:12"
  hebrew="וּכָלוּל בְּאוֹתָהּ הַצּוּרָה כֹּחַ הַחֹמֶר דְּהַתַּחְתּוֹן גַּם כֵּן אֶלָּא שֶׁמִּתְּחִלָּה הִיא כְּלוּלָה שָׁם רַק בְּכֹחַ וְאַחַר כָּךְ יוֹצֵא מִשָּׁם מִן הַכֹּחַ אֶל הַפּוֹעֵל לִמְצִיאוּת גָּמוּר גַּם כֵּן"
  translation="And contained within that *tzurah* is also the potential for matter of the lower plane — albeit that initially it is included there only *in potentia*. This subsequently emerges from potential to actual, as actual existence as well."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:13"
  hebrew="וַהֲרֵי הֵם שְׁנֵיהֶם מִשֹּׁרֶשׁ אֶחָד וּלְכָךְ הֵם מְיֻחָדִים זֶה בָּזֶה לְעוֹלָם כִּי מִתְּחִלָּה בַּשֹּׁרֶשׁ כָּלוּל הַחֹמֶר בַּצּוּרָה וְאַחַר כָּךְ כְּשֶׁנִּשְׁלְמוּ לִבְחִינַת מְצִיאוּת הָרָאוּי לַתַּחְתּוֹן מִתְלַבֶּשֶׁת הַצּוּרָה בְּהַחֹמֶר וְהֵם אֶחָד לְעוֹלָם"
  translation="Thus both *tzurah* and matter are from a single source, which is why they are always united with one another — since initially, in their root, matter was included in *tzurah*. But afterwards, when it became an entity befitting that lower plane, the *tzurah* becomes vested in the matter and they are one forever."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:14"
  hebrew="וּבֵאוּר הַדְּבָרִים הוּא כִּי הִנֵּה כָּל עוֹלָם תַּחְתּוֹן הוּא מִשְׁתַּלְשֵׁל וְנַעֲשָׂה כָּל מְצִיאוּתוֹ מֵהָעוֹלָם הָעֶלְיוֹן שֶׁעָלָיו שֶׁהוּא קוֹדֵם אֵלָיו בַּזְּמַן וּבַמַּעֲלָה"
  translation="The explanation is this: every lower world devolves from — and its entire existence is produced by — the world above it, by the plane of existence that precedes it in time and superiority."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:15"
  hebrew="וְסֵדֶר הַהִשְׁתַּלְשְׁלוּת הוּא כִּי מִתְפַּשֵּׁט מִתְּחִלָּה אוֹרוֹ וְעַצְמוּתוֹ הָרָאוּי לִהְיוֹת שֹׁרֶשׁ לְמַדְרֵגַת הַתַּחְתּוֹן הִנֵּה מִתְפַּשֵּׁט וְיוֹרֵד בַּמָּקוֹם הֶעָתִיד לַתַּחְתּוֹן וְעַל יְדֵי יְרִידָתוֹ וְרִחוּקוֹ מִמְּקוֹרוֹ הִנֵּה נִתְחַשֵּׁךְ וְנִתְעַבָּה קְצָת"
  translation="This *hishtalshelut* sequence occurs because initially the light and essence of the higher plane that was fit to become the source of the lower plane spreads out and descends to the future place of the lower plane, and by descending and becoming distant from its source it becomes dimmed and somewhat 'thicker' — i.e. coarser and less permeable to the light."
>

'Thicker' is not meant literally. It signifies a qualitative reduction and degrading of the light, much like physical light emerging from a semi-opaque filter — it is still light but much dimmer and weaker than it was before.

</Passage>

<Passage

/>

<Passage

<Passage

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:20"
  hebrew="וְהַשְּׁלִישִׁי הוּא כִּי הִנֵּה בַּאֲשֶׁר שֶׁכָּל אוֹתוֹ הָאוֹר שֶׁנִּתְפַּשֵּׁט הִנֵּה הוּא רַק כְּדֵי לְהֵעָשׂוֹת לִבְחִינַת שֹׁרֶשׁ לַתַּחְתּוֹן הִנֵּה מְקוֹמוֹ וְעִקָּרוֹ הוּא רַק לְמַעְלָה בְּהֶעֱלֵם הָעֶלְיוֹן"
  translation="3) The light spreads forth entirely for the purpose of becoming a source for the lower plane — although its proper place and primary nature is only above, in the concealed upper plane."
>

The spreading forth of the light from the higher plane to the lower is for the sake of the lower plane. Yet in itself the light 'belongs' above, as the Baal HaLeshem goes on to explain.

</Passage>

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:21"
  hebrew="כִּי כֵן הוּא מֻכְרָח לְכָל עוֹלָם וּמְצִיאוּת לִהְיוֹת לוֹ שֹׁרֶשׁ בְּמָקוֹם עֶלְיוֹן שֶׁמִּשָּׁם שׁוֹאֵב חַיּוּתוֹ תָּמִיד וְהָיָה כָּל הִתְפַּשְּׁטוּתוֹ אֲשֶׁר נִתְפַּשֵּׁט בִּמְקוֹם הֶעָתִיד לַתַּחְתּוֹן רַק כְּדֵי לְהִצְטַמְצֵם לְמַדְרֵגָתוֹ לִהְיוֹת שֹׁרֶשׁ עֶלְיוֹן אֵלָיו"
  translation="In the Divine plan it is necessary that every world and existing entity has its source in the plane above, from whence it draws its life force — so that the entire spreading forth of the higher level to the future place of the lower level is only so that it can become diminished in its descent, and in this way become its supernal source."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:22"
  hebrew="וּמְקוֹמוֹ הָעִקָּרִי הוּא רַק לְמַעְלָה בְּהֶעֱלֵם הָעֶלְיוֹן עַצְמוֹ וְלָכֵן הוּא כִּי אַחַר שֶׁנִּתְפַּשֵּׁט וְנִצְטַמְצֵם וְנַעֲשָׂה רָאוּי לִהְיוֹת שֹׁרֶשׁ לַתַּחְתּוֹן הִנֵּה הוּא אֵינוֹ עוֹמֵד שָׁם עוֹד כְּלָל אֶלָּא שֶׁחוֹזֵר וְנִסְתַּלֵּק וְעוֹלֶה לְמַעְלָה וְנִשְׁאָר שָׁם לְעוֹלָם"
  translation="Its primary place is only above in the concealed upper plane itself. Therefore, after it spreads forth and becomes diminished so that it is suitable as a source for the lower, it does not remain there at all. Instead it returns and is withdrawn above, where it always remains."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:23"
  hebrew="אָמְנָם בְּחִינַת הָעֲבִיּוּת שֶׁאָמַרְנוּ שֶׁנַּעֲשָׂה בּוֹ עַל יְדֵי יְרִידָתוֹ הִנֵּה זֶה נִשְׁאָר לְמַטָּה וְאֵין לוֹ כֹּחַ לַעֲלוֹת לְמַעְלָה מֵאַחַר שֶׁנִּתְעַבָּה וְהִנֵּה נִפְרָד אָז הָעֲבִיּוּת מֵהָאוֹר וְנִשְׁאָר הִיא לְמַטָּה וְהָאוֹר חוֹזֵר וְעוֹלֶה לְמַעְלָה"
  translation="However, the aspect of 'thickening' that we mentioned — which is caused by its descent — remains below. This diminished light has no power to ascend above since it has become thickened, which separates it from the original light. And therefore it remains below while the undiminished light returns and ascends above."
>

The aspect of light that was diminished and thickened remains below while the undiminished light is retracted and remains above. The light that remains below is called the *reshimu*[^6] or *roshem ha-ohr* — the residue of the light, as will be explained.

</Passage>

<Passage
  marker="§ 2:24"
  hebrew="וְדַע עוֹד כִּי עִקַּר בְּחִינַת הָעֲבִיּוּת שֶׁאָמַרְנוּ שֶׁנַּעֲשֶׂה בְּהָאוֹר עַל יְדֵי יְרִידָתוֹ וְרִחוּקוֹ מִמְּקוֹרוֹ הִנֵּה הוּא נַעֲשֶׂה עַל יְדֵי כֹּחַ סוֹד הַצִּמְצוּם הַמְּבֹאָר בְּדִבְרֵי הָאֲרִיזָ״ל שֶׁהוּא הַמָּקוֹם לְכָל הָעוֹלָמוֹת כֻּלָּם"
