***Shaar* 1 Chapter 6**

**His Revelation within Names and Worlds is Concealed**

<Passage
  marker="§ 6:1"
  hebrew="וַהֲרֵי נִתְבָּאֵר שֶׁכָּל גִּלּוּיָיו כֻּלָּם ר״ל גַּם מַה שֶׁנִּגְלָה אֵלֵינוּ בְּהַשָּׂגָה חוּשְׁיִּית וְאָמַר לָנוּ שֶׁהוּא תֹּאַר דְּיוֹקְנוֹ עַצְמוֹ"
  translation="It was explained above that all of His revelations, i.e. including that which is revealed to us through sensory perception, are a portrayal of His likeness, we are told."
>

This was explained in chapter 4 from the statement of the Sages of the *Midrash Bamidbar Rabbah* chap. 2: In earlier generations, anyone who pointed with his finger to the 'representation' of the king was put to death, but now even young children go to the House of Study and point with their finger to Him, i.e. to the expressions of the four-letter Name in the Torah.

</Passage>

<Passage
  marker="§ 6:2"
  hebrew="וְהוּא הַד׳ אוֹתִיּוֹת דְּהַשֵּׁם הוי״ה ב״ה וְכֵן כָּל הַשֵּׁמוֹת הַקְּדוֹשִׁים כֻּלָּם וְכָל הַתּוֹרָה כּוּלָהּ שֶׁהִיא כּוּלָהּ שְׁמוֹ יִתְבָּרַךְ וְהֵם כֻּלָּם דְּיּוּקָן גִּלּוּיָיו מַמָּשׁ אֲשֶׁר נִתְגַּלָּה בָּהֶם אֲמִתָּתוֹ הַנֶּעֱלָמָה עֲצְמוֹ ע״י הָאֲצִילוּת"
  translation="This portrayal of His likeness is by way of the four letters of the blessed Name Havaye, and all of the holy names, and the entire Torah – which in its entirety is His blessed name – and they are indeed all representations of His revelations in which His hidden truth becomes revealed via the world of Atzilut."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 6:3"
  hebrew="עכ״ז הִנֵּה אוֹר קְדֻשָּׁתוֹ עַצְמוֹ הַשּׁוֹרֶה בָּהֶם אֲשֶׁר עַל יָדוֹ נַעֲשִׂים הֵם לְדִּיוֹקְנוֹ הִנֵּה נִשְׁאָר אֵלֵינוּ עֲדַיִן נֶעֱלָם בְּתַכְלִית הַהֶעֱלֶם כְּמוֹ זוּלַת גִּלּוּיָיו"
  translation="Nevertheless, the light of His holiness itself abiding within them – within the holy names – which fashions them into His likeness, is still absolutely hidden from us, just as if there had been no revelation."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 6:4"
  hebrew="וְכָל מַה שֶׁנִּתְגַּלָּה בָּהֶם אֵלֵינוּ הוּא לְפִי הַשָּׂגָתֵנוּ רַק בִּגְזֵירָה שֶׁכָּךְ אָמַר לָנוּ וְכָךְ צִוָּה לָנוּ"
  translation="And all that is revealed within them – within the holy names – to us according to our comprehension is only by Divine decree, for that is what He told us and this is what He commanded,"
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 6:5"
  hebrew="וּכְמוֹ שֶׁאָמַר הוּא ית״ש זֶה שְׁמִי וְזֶה זָכְרִי וְכֵן צִוָּה לָנוּ וכמ״ש בְּפ׳ תָּבֹא לִירְאָה אֶת הַשֵּׁם הַנִּכְבָּד"
  translation="as He stated, 'This is My name… this is My remembrance'[^8] and this is what He commanded us, as it states in *parshat Tavo*[^9] '…to be in awe of this honored Name.'"
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 6:6"
  hebrew="וְאֵלָיו הוּא כָּל הָעֲבוֹדָה כְּמוֹ שֶׁנִּתְבָּאֵר לְקָמָן שַׁעַר ז׳ פ״ו וְהוּא שֵׁם הָעֶצֶם כִּי אֲמִתָּתוֹ הַנַּעֲלָמָה עַצְמוֹ נִתְגַּלָּה ע״י הָאֲצִילוּת בְּהַשֵּׁם הַזֶּה"
  translation="And to His Name is all of our service, as will be explained further on, in Shaar 7 chapter 6. This is the Essential Name, for His hidden true Being is revealed via Atzilut in this Name."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 6:7"
  hebrew="אֲבָל כ״ז הוּא רַק בִּידִיעָה וְלֹא בְּהַשָּׂגָה כִּי הוּא נֶעֱלָם בְּתַכְלִית הַהֶעֱלֵם מִלְּהַשִּׂיג אֶת זֶה בְּהַשָּׂגָתֵנוּ אֵיךְ שֶׁמִּתְיַחֵס אוֹר עַצְמוּתוֹ לְהִתְגַּלָּה בְּהַשֵּׁם הַזֶּה וְלוֹמַר שֶׁהוּא עַצְמוֹ וְהוּא הוּא"
  translation="But again all of this is merely in our awareness – we are aware that this is so – but not in our comprehension, because it is absolutely concealed from our intellectual grasp how His essential light becomes revealed in this Name, for us to state that He Himself is it – that He is His name."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 6:8"
  hebrew="אַךְ עכ״פ הוּא כִּי הָאֱמֶת כֵּן הוּא שֶׁהוּא הוּא כִּי כֵּן אָמַר לָנוּ וְכֵן צִוָּה לָנוּ כנז׳ וְהַשֵּׁם הַזֶּה הוּא אֲמִתַּת עַצְמוּתוֹ עַצְמוֹ שֶׁנִּתְגַּלָּה בּוֹ ע״י הָאֲצִילוּת וְהוּא אֱלֹהֵינוּ אֵין עוֹד[^10]"
  translation="But nevertheless, this is the truth – He is His Name, for this is what He told us and that this is what He commanded us, as mentioned earlier. This Name is His true essential being revealed in this Name via Atzilut, and He is our G-d and there is none other."
/>
