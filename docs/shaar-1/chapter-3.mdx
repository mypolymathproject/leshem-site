**שער א פרק ג**

***Shaar* 1 Chapter 3**

**Derivative Names and Titles**

<Passage

<Passage

  translation="– meaning to say, when His true hidden essence becomes revealed and can be called by this Name – this revelation in which He is revealed is only by way of finite existence."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 3:3"
  hebrew="וְהֲיְינוּ ע״י הַמְּצִיאוּת דְכָּל הַנֶּאֱצָלִים אֲשֶׁר מֵהַצִּמְצוּם וּלְמַטָּה שֶׁעַל יָדָם נִתְגַּלָּה לְהִקָּרֵא הוּא עַצְמוֹ בְּשֵׁם הויה ב״ה"
  translation="I.e. His Name is revealed via the existence of the emanated worlds post-*tzimtzum* and on. It is through them that He becomes revealed so that He Himself can be called Havaye, may He be blessed."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 3:4"
  hebrew="אָמְנָם הַגִּלּוּי הַזֶּה הוּא גִּלּוּי כְּלָלִי וּמִסְתַּעֵף לִפְרָטִים רַבִּים שֶׁהֵם כָּל הַשֵּׁמוֹת וְכִנּוּיִם כֻּלָּם הַנִּמְסָר לָנוּ בתוה״ק שֶׁבִּכְתָב ושׁבע״פ כְּכָל הַמְּבֹאָר בְּדִבְרֵי חַכְמֵי הָאֱמֶת"
  translation="However, this revelation is a general revelation that branches out into many details, which are all the holy Names and designations that have been conveyed to us in the holy Torah, both Written and Oral. As all this is explained in the words of the Sages of Truth – a term reserved for the sages of the Kabbalah."
>

There are seven holy names appearing in the Written Torah (i.e. Scripture), often referred to as 'the seven names which it is forbidden to erase.'[^1]

</Passage>

<Passage
  marker="§ 3:5"
  hebrew="וְכֻלָּם הֵם מִסְתַּעֲפִים מֵהַשֵּׁם הויה ב״ה כִּי כֻּלָּם הֵם לְפִי הוֹרָאַת גִּלּוּיָיו הַפְּרָטִים אֲשֶׁר נִתְגַּלָּה בְּהַמְּצִיאוֹת"
  translation="But all of them branch out from the blessed Name Havaye, because all of them are according to the specific revelations revealed within finite existence."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 3:6"
  hebrew="הֵן בִּפְרָטֵי מְצִיאוּתָם גּוּפָהּ וְהֵן בִּפְרָטֵי תַּהֲלוּכוֹת עִנְיְנֵי הַנְהָגָתָם"
  translation="This is the case with regard to the details of their existence itself, and with regard to their specific manner of conduct"
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 3:7"
  hebrew="וְהוּא מָה שֶׁנּוֹדַע מֵאֳמְרַם זַ״ל בשׁמו״ר פ״ג סִימָן ו׳ לְפִי מַעֲשַׂי אֲנִי נִקְרָא"
  translation="– as is known from the statement of our Sages in *Shemot Rabba* 3:6: 'According to My deeds I am called…'"
>

The Midrash explains that after God gave Moses the mission to liberate the Israelites from Egypt, Moses asked: "When I come to the Israelites to tell them, 'the God of your forefathers sent me to you' they will say to me, 'What is His name?' What should I then answer them?" (Exodus 3:13). The Midrash goes on to give God's response, the latter part of which the Baal HaLeshem quotes here: "My \[ineffable\] Name you wish to know? *According to My deeds I am called.*"[^3] We will refer to the latter as names and 'titles' or 'designations' – i.e. the names and titles (such as 'the Merciful One, the Almighty etc.) used in reference to God. When 'Name' is capitalized it refers to the ineffable Name (י-ה-ו-ה or *Havaya*).

</Passage>

<Passage
  marker="§ 3:8"
  hebrew="וְנִמְצָא וְנַעֲשָׂה כָּל שֵׁם וְשֵׁם לְפִי גִּלּוּיוֹ שֶׁבְּכָל פְּרָט כִּי הַשֵּׁם וְהַגִּלּוּי אֶחָד הוּא שֶׁהַשֵּׁם עַצְמוֹ הוּא הַגִּלּוּי"
  translation="So it turns out that each name is fashioned according to its specific revelation in every detail, because the name and the revelation are one and the same. For the name itself *is* the revelation."
>

The above applies only to the other names, not to the ineffable Name, as will be clarified shortly.

</Passage>

<Passage
  marker="§ 3:9"
  hebrew="וְכָל אֵלּוּ הַשֵּׁמוֹת וְהַגִּלּוּיִם הַפְּרָטִיִּים הֵם כֻּלָּם מִסְתַּעֲפִים וְיוֹצְאִים מִשְּׁמוֹ וְגִלּוּיוֹ הַכְּלָלִי שֶׁהוּא הַשֵּׁם הויה ב״ה כִּי הֵם רַק פְּרָטֵי גִּלּוּיָיו עַצְמוֹ כנז׳"
  translation="All of these names and individual revelations – the ones that are according to His deeds – all of them branch out and emerge from His Name and overarching revelation,[^4] the Name Havaye. For they are merely the specific revelations of the ineffable Name itself, as mentioned."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 3:10"
  hebrew="וְהִנֵּה נִתְגַּלָּה בכ״ז ע״י הַמְּצִיאוּת דְּכָל הַנֶּאֱצָלִים אֲשֶׁר מֵהַצִּמְצוּם וּלְמַטָּה"
  translation="Yet nevertheless they are revealed through the limited existence of all the emanated worlds and levels from the *tzimtzum* and on."
>

See our explanation of the *tzimtzum* according to Rabbi Elyashiv in *shaar* 1, chap. 2.

</Passage>

<Passage
  marker="§ 3:11"
  hebrew="כִּי עי״ז נִתְגַּלָּה לְהִקָּרֵא בְּשֵׁם הויה ב״ה וְכֵן בְּכָל הַשֵּׁמוֹת וְכִנּוּיִם הַמִּסְתַּעֲפִים מִמֶּנּוּ"
  translation="For it is by way of this *tzimtzum* that He becomes revealed to be called Havaye (may He be blessed) and all the other names and titles that stem from it from the ineffable Name Havaye."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 3:12"
  hebrew="וְהֵם כָּל הַתּוֹאֲרִים כֻּלָּם אֲשֶׁר עָלָיו ית״ש שֶׁהוּא עַצְמוֹ נִקְרָא וּמְתֹאָר בָּהֶם ע״פ גִּלּוּיָיו שֶׁבְּאֲצִילוּת"
  translation="These are all the descriptive terms or titles for Him, may He be blessed, by which He is called and described – according to what He reveals in the world of Atzilut."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 3:13"
  hebrew="וְהִנֵּה הָיָה כָּל אוֹתָן הַגִּלּוּיִם בַּאֲצִילוּת לְפִי צֹרֶךְ הַמְּצִיאוּת דָּהָעוֹלָמוֹת בי״ע כָּל מָה שֶׁנִּצְרַךְ לִמְצִיאוּתָם וְהַנְהָגָתָם"
  translation="Now all of these revelations of the various names and titles in Atzilut are according to the needs of the existential worlds – i.e. the finite worlds of *BiYA* \[Beriah, Yetzirah and Asiyah\], in contrast with the emanated world of Atzilut and above."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 3:14"
  hebrew="וְזֶהוּ שֶׁאָמְרוּ בְּרע״מ פִּנְחָס רנ״ז ב׳ קֹדֶם דְּבָרָא עַלְמָא אִתְקְרֵי הוּא בְּכָל אִלֵּין דָּרְגִין עַל שֵׁם בִּרְיָין דַּהֲווֹ עֲתִידִין לְהִבָּרְאוֹת"
  translation="This is what the *Zohar* III in *Raya Mehemna*[^5] *Pinchas* 257b states: 'Before He created the world He was called all of those levels in the name of the future creation.'"
>

The *Zohar* here addresses the question as to whether the various names and titles indicate a change in the *Ein Sof* from one state to another – e.g. from judgment to mercy, which would contradict the principles of G-d's immutability and His unity. The *Zohar* answers that even prior to Creation He is called all of these different names and titles etc., not because they actually apply to Him, but because it arose in His will to create the various worlds where these terms would apply in the future. It is only because of the future creation that these terms come into being.[^6]

</Passage>

  translation="These are all the revelations in Atzilut that were revealed there according to the order that He wanted in order to later on establish the existential worlds of *BiYA* – Beriah, Yetzirah and Asiyah."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 3:16"
  hebrew="וְאָמַר שָׁם הָרע״מ עוֹד דְּאִי לֲאו בִּרְיָין דַּהֲווֹ דְּעַלְמָא אַמַּאי אִתְקְּרוֹ רַחוּם, דַּיָין? אֶלָּא עַל שֵׁם בִּרְיָין דַּעְתִּידִין"
  translation="The *Raya Mehemna* ibid. states further that if not for the creations that would come to be in the world, why would He be called 'the Merciful One, or Judge?' Rather, all of the names and titles are for the sake of future creations."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 3:17"
  hebrew="דְּאִילוּלָא שֶׁהִמְצִיא אֶת הָעוֹלָמוֹת בי״ע הֲרֵי הָיָה כָּל גִּלּוּיָיו שֶׁבַּאֲצִילוּת לְלֹא צֹרֶךְ וּבְגִין דָּא כָּל שְׁמָּהָן אִנּוּן כִּנּוּיִין דִּלְיֵהּ עַל שֵׁם עוֹבְדִין דִּלְיֵהּ שֶׁכָּל הַשֵּׁמוֹת שֶׁבַּאֲצִילוּת שֶׁהֵם גִּלּוּיָיו הֵם רַק בִּשְׁבִיל הבי״ע ע״פ מָה שֶׁרָאוּי לָהֶם"
  translation="Were it not for the worlds of *BiYA* – Beriah, Yetzirah and Asiyah – that He brought forth, all of the revelations in Atzilut would have been unnecessary. Therefore, all the holy names, His titles so to speak, are given in reference to His deeds, since all the holy names in Atzilut, which are His revelations, are only for the sake of the worlds of *BiYA*, in the manner that is appropriate for each of them."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 3:18"
  hebrew="אָמְנָם כָּל הַגִּלּוּיִם הַלָּלוּ הוּא רַק מֵעֵת הַצִּמְצוּם וּלְהָלְאָה וְכֵן רַק מֵהַצִּמְצוּם וּלְמַטָּה שֶׁהוּא בְּכָל הָאֲצִילוּת כֻּלּוֹ אֲבָל לֹא קֹדֶם הָאֲצִילוּת וְלֹא לְמַעְלָה מֵאַצִּילוּת"
  translation="However, all of these revelations are only from the time of the *tzimtzum* and on, and only from the *tzimtzum* and below – which is the entire emanated world of Atzilut, but not prior to the emanation of Atzilut, and not above it, on a higher level than Atzilut."
>

All of the holy names and titles become manifested only after the *tzimtzum,* and only in the world of Atzilut, not above it. The *Meir HaLeshem,*[^7] one of the commentaries on this work, notes that the Baal HaLeshem seems to be making a distinction here between time ("not prior to…") and space ("not above it"), and he is puzzled as to the reason for the distinction.

However, the concept of time itself in the normal meaning of the word is not relevant in Atzilut, just the concept of space per se is not relevant. Perhaps he means what is called in Midrash *seder zemanim.*[^8]

</Passage>

  marker="§ 3:19"

  translation="It transpires therefore that each and every one of His revelations, which are all the names and titles discussed previously, all of them are only revealed by way of the existence of the world of Atzilut. And from them – the revelations in Atzilut – they are drawn down into the existential worlds of *BiYA*, as appropriate for them."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 3:20"
  hebrew="אָמְנָם כָּל הַגִּלּוּיִם הַלָּלוּ שֶׁהֵם כָּל הַשֵּׁמוֹת וְהַכִּנּוּיִם כֻּלָּם הִנֵה הֵם מָה שֶׁנִּתְגַּלָּה הוּא עַצְמוֹ בָּהֶם ע״י הָאֲצִילוּת וְנִקְרָא הוּא עַצְמוֹ בָּהֶם כֻּלָּם ע״י הָאֲצִילוּת"
  translation="However, all of these revelations – each and every one of the names and titles – are the way in which He Himself is revealed in them via Atzilut. And He Himself is called by all of them via the emanated world of Atzilut."
>

As the Baal HaLeshem indicated before (from the Midrash) "According to My deeds **I** am called…" – in other words, it is Me who is acting through these names etc. via their source in Atzilut, as he goes on to explain:

</Passage>

<Passage
  marker="§ 3:21"
  hebrew="וְהִנֵה הֵם כֻּלָּם רַק מֵעֵת שֶׁעָלָה בִּרְצוֹנוֹ לְהִמָּצֵא בְּהַמְּצִיאוּת דְּהַאֲצִילוּת וְלְהִתְגַּלָּה וּלְהַמְצִיא עַל יְדֵיהֶם אֶת כָּל הָעוֹלָמוֹת בי״ע"
  translation="Now all of these names are only from the time that it arose in His will to be found within[^9] the existence of Atzilut and to be revealed there, and to bring forth all the worlds of *BiYA* thereby."
/>
