***Shaar* 1 Chapter 7**

**His Essence is Revealed in and until Atzilut; The Primary Revelation is through Torah**

<Passage
  marker="§ 7:1"
  hebrew="וְהִנֵּה סִבַּת הַדָּבָר מָה שְׂאוֹר קְדֻשָּׁתוֹ ית״ש הוּא נֶעֱלַם מִלְּהַשִּׂיג בּוֹ מְאוּמָה גַּם אַחֵר שֶׁנִּתְגַּלָּה בְּכָל גִּלּוּיָיו הַנִּזְכָּרִים הִנֵּה הוּא בְּסִבַת מָה שֶׁאָמַרְנוּ לְעֵיל פ״ב כִּי נִתְצַמְצֵם עַצְמוּתוֹ ית״ש וּמִסְתַּיֵּם בַּסּוֹף הָאֲצִילוּת"
  translation="The reason that the light of His holiness is hidden from comprehension and cannot be grasped at all – even after being revealed in all the revelations mentioned above – the reason for this is as we stated above in chapter 2: because He constricted Himself in a way that these revelations come to an end at the lowest rungs of Atzilut."
>

Since even the lowest levels of Atzilut, where the light is somewhat dimmed, transcend our limited comprehension, how much more so is the light that is revealed in Atzilut beyond our comprehension.

</Passage>

<Passage
  marker="§ 7:2"
  hebrew="וְכָל מָה שֶׁמִּתְפַּשֵּׁט בָּאוֹרוֹ וּקְדֻשָּׁתוֹ לְמַטָּה הוּא רַק ע״י מְסַכִים וּלְבוּשִׁים וְהוּא רַק תּוֹלְדוֹת הָאוֹר וְתַמְצִית הֶאָרָה לְבַד וּכְנָ״ל פ״ב"
  translation="Anything that suffuses His light and holiness below is only via screening and cloaking, so that what is revealed is merely a product of the light and only a diminishing or attenuation of the radiance, as explained in chapter 2."
>

In order for the light to descend to *BiYA* it must be dimmed and attenuated. This process is akin to covering or cloaking it in garments which obscure the light.

</Passage>

<Passage
  marker="§ 7:3"
  hebrew="וְאוֹתָהּ הַתַּמְצִית הֶאָרָה הוּא עוֹמֵד בִּפְנִימִיּוּת כָּל הָעוֹלָמוֹת בי״ע בִּבְחִי׳ נְשָׁמָה לַנְּשָׁמָה וְהוּא אוֹר שְׁכִינָתוֹ יִתְבָּרֵךְ הַשּׁוֹכֵן בְּתוֹךְ כָּל הָעוֹלָמוֹת בי״ע ע״י הַתּוֹרָה וְיִשְׂרָאֵל"
  translation="Now this diminishing of the radiance suffuses the inner core of the worlds of BiYA as the aspect of the soul of the soul,[^1] of those worlds, which is His Shechinah, may He be blessed, within the worlds of BiYA, drawn down there by Torah and the Jewish People."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 7:4"
  hebrew="וְאוֹתוֹ הָאוֹר הוּא מְקוֹמוֹ שֶׁל עוֹלָם הַמְּהַוֶּה אֶת הַכָּל וְנוֹשֵׂא אֶת כָּל וּמַנְהִיג אֶת הַכָּל תָּמִיד ע״י קִשּׁוּרָהּ וְאַחְדוּתָהּ בְּעֶצֶם הָאוֹר הָעִקָּרִי אֲשֶׁר לְמַעְלָה בַּאֲצִילוּת"
  translation="And that light itself is the place[^2] of the world, and brings everything into being, and sustains and leads everything always, by way of its bond and unification with the essence of the primary light that transcends Atzilut."
>

That abridged extract from the supernal radiance is itself the life force of the lower worlds.

</Passage>

<Passage
  marker="§ 7:5"
  hebrew="וְלָכֵן אֵין גִּלּוּי בַּהַשָּׂגָה לְכָל הָעוֹלָמוֹת בי״ע כֻּלָּן בְּעַצְמוּתוֹ ית״ש מְאוּמָה אַחֲרֵי שֶׁגַּם אוֹר שְׁכִינָתוֹ ית״ש הַשּׁוֹכֵן בָּהֶם הוּא רַק תַּמְצִית הֶאָרָה מָאוֹר שֶׁל מַעְלָה"
  translation="And therefore there is no revelation at all of His blessed essence that can be grasped in any of the lower worlds of BiYA, since even the light of His blessed Shechinah that resides within them is only a diminished extract of the Supernal Light."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 7:6"
  hebrew="וְגַם זֶה הוּא רַק בִּפְנִימִית פְּנִימִיּוּתָם שֶׁהוּא בִּבְחִי׳ נָשְׁמָה לִנְשָׁמָה שֶׁהוּא לְמַעְלָה מִמַּדְרֵגַת הַנִּבְרָאִים לְגַמְרֵי כִּי כְּמַדְרֵגַת הַנִּבְרָאִים כֻּלָּם הוּא רַק מֵהַנְּשָׁמָה וּלְמַטָּה כְּמוֹ שֶׁיִּתְבָּאֵר בע״ה בַּשְּׁעָרִים הַבָּאִים"
  translation="And even that diminished extract is only in their innermost being, which is the soul of their souls, transcending the level of created being altogether. This is because the level of all created beings is from the level of soul called neshama[^3] and below, as will be explained in forthcoming chapters, G-d willing."
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 7:7"

<Passage

  hebrew="וְכֻלָּם עוֹנִים וְאוֹמְרִים בָּרוּךְ כְּבוֹד ה׳ מִמְּקוֹמוֹ מִמָּקוֹם שֶׁהוּא כִּי מְקוֹמוֹ לֵיכָּא דֵּידַע לֵיהּ"
  translation="And they all answer, 'Blessed is G-d's glory from its place' (Ezekiel 3:12) – from whatever place, for His place is unknown, as the Talmud states in Chagiga 13b[^5] and Zohar I, 103a.[^6]"
/>

<Passage
  marker="§ 7:9"
  hebrew="אָמְנָם מָה שֶׁיֵּשׁ לָנוּ גִּלּוּי מִמֶּנּוּ עַצְמוֹ ג״כ ר״ל זוּלַת פְּעֻלּוֹתָיו גּוּפָהּ הִנֵה הוּא בִּרְצוֹנוֹ הַמִּתְפַּשֵּׁט בהבי״ע"
  translation="However, as for revelations to us of Himself – i.e. besides His actions themselves – it is all in the extension of His will into the worlds of BiYA.[^7]"
>

His wanting those worlds to exist is their very existence. Had He not wanted their existence they simply would not be at all.

</Passage>

<Passage

/>

<Passage

<Passage

/>

<Passage

<Passage

<Passage

<Passage

  translation="Thus He is revealed in His Will, and its revelation to us is from Him Himself – meaning to say, from His essence itself – apart from His revelation through His actions of bringing everything into being, including the existence of the worlds of Beriah, Yetzirah and Asiyah. >

Although we stated before that the Almighty is revealed in the act of Creation, this is merely a secondary revelation. The primary revelation is in Torah and *mitzvot*.

</Passage>

<Passage

  translation="There in Atzilut His essence is revealed in the entire will He revealed to us throughout Torah and the mitzvot. And similarly regarding the Will that was activated in the process of creating all beings created in the world of Beriah, formed in the world of Yetzirah, and made in the world of Asiyah – from the Torah itself,[^15] since all of the worlds were made from the Torah, as the Zohar declares."
/>

  translation="Now since His entire will was revealed to us in it – in the Torah – the Torah is therefore the spreading forth of His very light. Therefore, one who cleaves to His Will, cleaves to Him Himself, and this is what the verse states, 'And you who cleave to God your Lord…' as mentioned."
